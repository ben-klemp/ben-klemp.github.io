Thesis Statement:

A thesis statement states both the writer's focused subject and their attitude or opinion.

  • Is it a complete sentence?
  • Is every word clear, precies, and effective?
  • What evidence must I use to support my thesis?

Conclusion:

Your conclusion should be strong, interesting, and convincing. You want it to satisfy your readers that you have done what your introduciton promised.
